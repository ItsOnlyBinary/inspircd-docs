### `/NICK <nick>`

Changes your nickname to `<nick>`.

You may also change your nick to your UUID by specifying a nickname of "0".

#### Example Usage

Changes your nick to Sadie:

```plaintext
/NICK Sadie
```

Changes your nick to your UUID:

```plaintext
/NICK 0
```
